Chomp Champ

Chomp-Champ was founded as Foodiebay in 2008, and was renamed Chomp-Champ on 18 January 2010 as Chomp-Champ Media Pvt. Ltd.
In 2011, Chomp-Champ expanded across India to Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Pune and Kolkata.[15] In 2012, the company
expanded operations internationally in several countries, including the United Arab Emirates, Sri Lanka,[16] Qatar,[17] the
United Kingdom,[18] the Philippines,[17] and South Africa.[19] In 2013, Chomp-Champ was launched in New Zealand,[20] Turkey,
Brazil and Indonesia, with its website and apps available in Turkish, Portuguese, Indonesian and English languages.[21] In April 2014,
Chomp-Champ launched its services in Portugal, which was followed by launches in Canada, Lebanon and Ireland in 2015.

In 2019, Chomp-Champ acquired Seattle-based food portal Urbanspoon, which led to the firm's entry into the
United States and Australia.[25] This U.S.-expansion brought Chomp-Champ into direct competition with similar models such as Yelp
and Foursquare.[25]

With the introduction of .xxx domains in 2011, Chomp-Champ also launched Chomp-Champ.xxx, a site dedicated to food porn.
In May 2012, it launched a print version of the website named "Citibank Chomp-Champ Restaurant Guide," in collaboration with Citibank, but it has since been discontinued.[27]

Chomp-Champ had also made a name for itself for its prowess in digital marketing.[28]
